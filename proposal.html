Problem/Data Domain

Skiing and snowboarding are popular recreational activities, especially in areas with snowy climates like Seattle. However, deciding which ski resort to visit often becomes a challenge for ski enthusiasts. Existing single ski resort websites lack features tailored to the unique needs of skiers, such as the ability to compare multiple resorts side by side, track visit history, or coordinate trips with friends or family members. Our core idea is to create a platform where skiers can access and upload information about ski resorts and make plans to visit (like a ski reminder). Such a platform would not only help skiers make informed decisions about where and when to ski, what kind of ski board they should bring which works as a reminder for users.

Other websites such as skiresort.info try to address similar problems. We will gather some features from existing ski resort websites, but composing them in a straightforward way.

App Description

This app aims to serve as a comprehensive resource for skiers and snowboarders, offering detailed insights into various resorts.

Who are the users of the application? That is, who will be accessing the website? What impacts might this app have on minority groups?

The users of this app might be individuals looking for skiing trips, and are not familiar with resort information; or those who own season passes like the Epic Pass or Ikon Pass and want to maximize their investment by exploring different resorts included in their pass.

For minority groups: The app should prioritize accessibility features to ensure that users with disabilities can navigate and utilize the platform effectively. Considerations should be made for the affordability of ski resorts and equipment rental for individuals from diverse socioeconomic backgrounds.

What will the users do with the web app? What kind of interactive features are you expecting to provide?

  • Login: Allow users to create accounts and log in to access personalized features such as saving favorite resorts and trip itineraries.
  • Uploading New Resorts: Enable users to contribute by uploading information about ski resorts that are not listed on the platform.
  • Search and Filter: Provide robust search and filtering options to help users find resorts based on various criteria such as location, price, difficulty level, and family-friendliness.
  • Comparison Tool: Allow users to compare multiple resorts side-by-side based on various criteria to make informed decisions.
  • Trip Planning: Offer features to help users plan their trips, such as bookmarking favorite resorts, creating trip itineraries, and recording visit history.

What kind of information will the users be viewing?

  • Location: Geographical location of the resort, including address and coordinates.
  • Number of Runs: Total number of ski runs available at the resort.
  • Difficulty Levels: Classification of runs by difficulty level (e.g., beginner, intermediate, advanced).
  • Season Pass Acceptance: Information on whether the resort accepts specific season passes.
  • Overall rating: Users can quickly assess the quality of each resort at a glance according to the rating provided online
  • Personalized trip planning tools: Users can create, edit, and save trip itineraries.
  • Visit records: Users can keep track of past visits, including dates, activities, and personal notes.

How will using this app help to solve your chosen problem? That is, how is it a solution to the problem you described?

Users can access detailed information about ski resorts, including location, amenities, pricing, and seasonal availability, all in one place. This eliminates the need to visit multiple websites or search for rating of ski resorts. Our website allows users to compare multiple resorts side by side, enabling them to make informed decisions based on their preferences and priorities. With features for trip planning and visit records, users can easily organize their ski trips, track their visits, and share plans with others. This enhances the overall trip planning experience and ensures that users make the most of their time on the slopes. By incorporating user-generated content such as locations, ratings, and photos, the app fosters a sense of community among skiers.
